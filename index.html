For the public,
this app aims to help you understand Dunman High School better! Where are the special places in this school, what are the atmosphere here etc!

For the students, just in case you have missed out some places in Dunman High, we have prepared this app to allow you to explore more!

Rules:
There will be total of 10 questions.
Choose the correct answer before time runs out!
Each correct answer will give you one point!
While wrong answer or no answer you will not get any point!
